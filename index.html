💰
See the true cost of your home
HomeBase tracks every real expense — utilities, insurance, maintenance, subscriptions — and shows where money is quietly leaking. Most homeowners overspend simply by never looking. A single internet negotiation often saves $200+ a year. HomeBase surfaces these moments automatically.
🔧
Stay ahead of repairs and surprises
The average furnace replacement costs $4,000–$8,000. The average roof, $12,000–$25,000. These aren't surprises — they're predictable. HomeBase tracks the age and condition of your major systems so you can budget ahead instead of scrambling when something fails at the worst possible moment.
📋
Your inspection report, in plain English
Home inspection reports are dense, jargon-heavy, and easy to misread. HomeBase reads yours and translates it into six clear sections with condition ratings — then offers to fill in your appliance and home profile data automatically. No more guessing what "evidence of deferred maintenance" actually means.
📁
All your home documents, finally organized
Warranty. Mortgage. Insurance policy. Inspection report. HOA documents. They're all important. They're usually scattered. HomeBase stores and analyzes them — surfacing key dates, risks, and opportunities so you don't have to dig through email folders when something goes wrong.
📊
A Home Health Score that actually means something
Unlike a vague "wellness score," HomeBase's Home Health Score is built on four real pillars: bill efficiency, payment reliability, home preparedness, and profile completeness. It updates in real time as you add data — and tells you exactly which pillar to focus on and why.
🔍
Smart insights without the financial advisor fees
HomeBase analyzes your bills against state averages, flags high rates, recommends when to refinance, and identifies negotiation opportunities — all without charging you by the hour. The Recommendations tab gives you specific, actionable steps tailored to your home and your state.
